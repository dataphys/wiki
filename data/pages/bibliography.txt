====== Bibliography ======

This bibliography is a **collection of academic work on data physicalization**. [[Contribute|You can help us extend it]].

For work that does not refer to data physicalization but describes technologies which may be used for that purpose, see [[technologies]]. For non-academic work, see the [[http://dataphys.org/list|list of physical visualizations]].
